
================================================================================
EXAMPLE 1
================================================================================
In a lush meadow, a camel named Jack and a horse named Henry 
started their journey together. They saw many different paths and 
explored each one with enthusiasm. One day, they both reached 
their destination, but they were both nervous about getting there 
early. As they approached the other side, they realized that they 
both needed to take off a few days earlier to get there.

Moral: With determination and preparation, we can overcome any 
       obstacle and reach our destination quickly.

================================================================================
EXAMPLE 2
================================================================================
A prince wanted to be a good knight in his kingdom. He had to wear
beautiful clothes, make fun of everyone, and get respect from the 
queen. But the princess told him that he should just be himself 
and that everyone respects him. So the prince became even more 
careful and helped those around him.

Moral: When we show love and care to others, it strengthens our 
       relationships and strengthens our kingdom.


================================================================================
EXAMPLE 3
================================================================================
A brave boy named Percy wanted to join the kingdom's most celebrated 
witch school. He met a shy but skilled witch named Sophia who taught 
him about courage. Percy realized that sometimes bravery is more 
important than magic.

Moral: Courage is the key to making your dreams come true.
